Welcome

Dhishna is the annual techfest of School Of Engineering,CUSAT. A myriad of events,mind boggling games,a cornucopia of challenges and competitions,exciting prizes and above all,an adventure that shouldn't be missed ! The 7 specialized branches of School Of Engineering,CUSAT merge together their talents,resources and hardwork,the building blocks for Dhishna. Breaking the usual norms of studies confined to textbooks,we aim to take learning to a whole new level. The perfect platform to launch your rockets,encompass your minds and explore the different niches of technology. Encouraging each and everyone of you,to step up,mould your thoughts and lives in your unique way,discover the tech lover in you ! We present to you, Dhishna.
